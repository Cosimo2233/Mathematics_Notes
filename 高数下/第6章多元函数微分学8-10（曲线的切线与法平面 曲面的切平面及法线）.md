## 一、空间曲线的切线与法平面

复习：平面曲线的切线与法线
已知平面光滑曲线 $y=f(x)$ 在点 $\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)$ 有
切线方程 $y-y_{0}=f^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)$
法线方程 $y-y_{0}=-\frac{1}{f^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)}\left(x-x_{0}\right)$
若平面光滑曲线方程为 $F(x, y)=0$ ，因 $\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=-\frac{F_{x}(x, y)}{F_{y}(x, y)}$故在点 $\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)$ 有
切线方程 $F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)+F_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right)=0$
法线方程 $F_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)-F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right)=0$

空间光滑曲线在点 $M$ 处的切线为此点处割线的极限位置．过点 $\boldsymbol{M}$ 与切线垂直的平面称为曲线在该点的法平面．

1．曲线方程为参数方程的情况
$\Gamma: \quad x=\varphi(t), y=\psi(t), z=\omega(t)$设 $t=t_{0}$ 对应 $M\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$
![](https://cdn.mathpix.com/cropped/506cd8c7-766e-49ef-bba2-3dc515d4631f-05.jpg?height=480&width=601&top_left_y=277&top_left_x=1806)
$t=t_{0}+\Delta t$ 对应 $M^{\prime}\left(x_{0}+\Delta x, y_{0}+\Delta y, z_{0}+\Delta z\right)$
割线 $M M^{\prime}$ 的方程 ：

$$
\frac{x-x_{0}}{\Delta x}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{\Delta y}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{\Delta z}
$$

上述方程之分母同除以 $\Delta t$ ，令 $\Delta t \rightarrow 0$ ，得切线方程 $\quad \frac{x-x_{0}}{\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{\psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{\omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}$

此处要求 $\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)$ 不全为 0 ，如个别为 0 ，则理解为分子为 0 。

切线的方向向量：

$$
\vec{T}=\left(\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\right)
$$

称为曲线的切向量。
![](https://cdn.mathpix.com/cropped/506cd8c7-766e-49ef-bba2-3dc515d4631f-06.jpg?height=677&width=486&top_left_y=207&top_left_x=1904)
$\vec{T}$ 也是法平面的法向量，因此得法平面方程

$$
\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)+\psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right)+\omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0
$$

说明：若引进向量函数 $\vec{r}(t)=(\varphi(t), \psi(t), \omega(t))$ ，则 $\Gamma$为 $\vec{r}(t)$ 的矢端曲线，而在 $t_{0}$ 处的导向量

$$
\overrightarrow{r^{\prime}}\left(t_{0}\right)=\left(\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\right)
$$

就是该点的切向量．

∴ 切线方程为 $x-x_{0}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{\varphi_{\left(x_{0}\right)}^{\prime}}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{\psi_{\left(x_{0}\right)}^{\prime}}$ ，

例3 求曲线 $y^{2}=2 m x, z^{2}=m-x$ 在 $\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 处的切线及法平面方程。
解 $\because\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=x \\ y^{2}=2 m x \\ z^{2}=m-x\end{array} \quad \therefore\left\{\begin{array}{l}x^{\prime}=1 \\ y^{\prime}=\frac{m}{y} \\ z^{\prime}=-\frac{1}{2 z}\end{array} \quad \therefore \vec{T}=\left\{1, \frac{m}{y_{0}},-\frac{1}{2 z_{0}}\right\}\right.\right.$

## 2．曲线为一般式的情况

光滑曲线 $\Gamma:\left\{\begin{array}{l}F(x, y, z)=0 \\ G(x, y, z)=0\end{array}\right.$
当 $J=\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)} \neq 0$ 时，$\Gamma$ 可表示为 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}y=\varphi(x) \\ z=\psi(x)\end{array}\right.$ ，且有

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}, \quad \frac{\mathrm{d} z}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)},
$$

曲线上一点 $M\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 处的切向量为
![](https://cdn.mathpix.com/cropped/506cd8c7-766e-49ef-bba2-3dc515d4631f-11.jpg?height=388&width=411&top_left_y=923&top_left_x=1973)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\vec{T} & =\left\{1, \varphi^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)\right\} \\
& =\left\{1,\left.\frac{1 \partial(F, G)}{J \partial(z, x)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{1 \partial(F, G)}{J \partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\right\}
\end{aligned}
$$

或 $\vec{T}=\left\{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\right\}$
则在点 $M\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 有
切线方程 $\frac{x-x_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}}$
法平面方程 $\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}\left(x-x_{0}\right)+\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}\left(y-y_{0}\right)$
$+\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0$

## 法平面方程

$$
\begin{aligned}
\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}\left(x-x_{0}\right) & +\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}\left(y-y_{0}\right) \\
& +\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0
\end{aligned}
$$

也可表为

$$
\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
x-x_{0} & y-y_{0} & z-z_{0} \\
F_{x}(M) & F_{y}(M) & F_{z}(M) \\
G_{x}(M) & G_{y}(M) & G_{z}(M)
\end{array}\right|=0
$$

## 二、曲面的切平面与法线

切平面的法向量与曲面上任一曲线的切向量垂直．法线是与切平面垂直的直线．
1．设曲面为 $F(x, y, z)=0$ ，求 $M_{0}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 处的切平面与法线

解．过 $M_{0}$ 任取一曲线（在曲面上），
设为 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\varphi(t) \\ y=\psi(t) \\ z=\omega(t)\end{array}\right.$
![](https://cdn.mathpix.com/cropped/506cd8c7-766e-49ef-bba2-3dc515d4631f-19.jpg?height=827&width=1006&top_left_y=888&top_left_x=1419)

该曲线在 $M_{0}$ 的切向量为 $\vec{T}=\left\{\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\right\}$
又 $F[\varphi(t), \psi(t), \omega(t)] \equiv 0$ ，
则 $F_{x} \varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)+F_{y} \psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)+F_{z} \omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)=0$ ．
即 $\left\{F_{x}, F_{y}, F_{z}\right\} \cdot\left\{\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\right\}=0$ ，
$\vec{n}=\left\{F_{x}, F_{y}, F_{z}\right\}$ 与切向量 $\vec{T}$ 垂直，$\vec{n}$ 即为切平面的法向量．
∴ 切平面为
$F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)+F_{y}\left(y-y_{0}\right)+F_{z}\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0$,
法线为 $\frac{x-x_{0}}{F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{F_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{F_{z}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}$ ．

切平面的法向量称为曲面的法向量

$$
\begin{aligned}
\vec{n} & =\left.\left\{F_{x}, F_{y}, F_{z}\right\}\right|_{\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)} \\
& =\left\{F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right), F_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right), F_{z}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\right\}
\end{aligned}
$$

2．设曲面为 $z=f(x, y)$ ，求 $M_{0}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 处的切平面与法线

解 设 $F(x, y, z)=f(x, y)-z$ ，

$$
\text { 则 } \vec{n}=\left.\left(f_{x}, f_{y},-1\right)\right|_{\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)} \text {, }
$$

切平面 ：$f_{x} \cdot\left(x-x_{0}\right)+f_{y} \cdot\left(y-y_{0}\right)-\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0$ ，
法线：$\frac{x-x_{0}}{f_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{f_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{-1}$ ．

## 注意：

（1）因为曲面在 $M_{0}$ 处的切平面方程为

竖坐标的增量 $z=f(x, y)$ 在 $\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)$ 的全微分，表示曲面 $z=f(x, y)$ 在点 $\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 处的切平面上的点的坚坐标的增量。
（2）若 $\alpha 、 \beta 、 \gamma$ 表示曲面的法向量的方向角，并假定法向量的方向是向上的，即使得它与 $z$ 轴的正向所成的角 $\gamma$ 是锐角，则法向量的方向余弦为对于 $z=f(x, y)$ ，有 $\vec{n}= \pm\left(f_{x}, f_{y},-1\right)$ ，

\begin{aligned}
& \cos \alpha=\frac{-f_{x}}{\sqrt{1+f_{x}^{2}+f_{y}^{2}}}, \\
& \cos \beta=\frac{-f_{y}}{\sqrt{1+f_{x}^{2}+f_{y}^{2}}}, \\
& \cos \gamma=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+f_{x}^{2}+f_{y}^{2}}} .
\end{aligned}
$$

（3）平面曲线 $F(x, y)=0$ 的法向量为 $\vec{n}=\left\{F_{x}, F_{y}\right\}$ ．
（4）对于曲线 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}F(x, y, z)=0 \\ G(x, y, z)=0\end{array}\right.$ ，

$$
\vec{n}_{1}=\left\{F_{x}, F_{y}, F_{z}\right\}, \quad \vec{n}_{2}=\left\{G_{x}, G_{y}, G_{z}\right\}
$$

则切向量为 $: \vec{T}=\vec{n}_{1} \times \vec{n}_{2}=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}\vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\ F_{x} & F_{y} & F_{z} \\ G_{x} & G_{y} & G_{z}\end{array}\right|$

$$

$$

即 $\quad 3 x^{2}-2 y^{3}+3 z^{2}=10$

令 $F(x, y, z)=3 x^{2}-2 y^{3}+3 z^{2}-10$
$\left.F_{x}\right|_{(\sqrt{3}, 1,-1)}=\left.6 x\right|_{(\sqrt{3}, 1,-1)}=6 \sqrt{3},\left.F_{y}\right|_{(\sqrt{3}, 1,-1)}=-\left.6 y^{2}\right|_{(\sqrt{3}, 1,-1)}=-6$,
